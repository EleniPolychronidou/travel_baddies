Weekend in Paris

Destination: Paris, France

Purpose: Recreation

Budget: 400€

Date: 3-5 April 2026

Description: Spend a delightful weekend exploring the heart of Paris, combining culture, food, and relaxation. Begin with a visit to the Eiffel Tower, where you can enjoy stunning panoramic views of the city. Wander through the Louvre Museum and admire masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. Take a stroll along the Seine River, exploring historic bridges and picturesque riverside spots, and stop at local cafés to enjoy freshly baked pastries, coffee, or a light lunch. Discover charming streets and boutique shops in neighborhoods like Le Marais, perfect for shopping or simply soaking up the Parisian atmosphere. End your weekend with a memorable dinner near the illuminated Eiffel Tower, enjoying fine French cuisine while experiencing the city’s romantic ambiance. This trip offers the perfect balance of sightseeing, culinary experiences, and leisurely moments, making it ideal for first-time visitors and lovers of Paris alike..
