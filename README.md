# Travel Buddy
## It's always better with more people

Travel Buddy is a platform that aims to connect people who do not want to travel solo and and are looking for travel companions. The platform falls into the category of social media. With its use, a travel-focused community is created, consisting of *travelers* and *travel agencies*. Its environment is simple, functional and user-friendly, allowing users to create profiles, propose or search for available trips and participate in groups of people with similar interests in just a few steps.
